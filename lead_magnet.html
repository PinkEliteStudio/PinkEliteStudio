Top 5 Supplement Types

  • Protein: Ideal for muscle growth and recovery, especially for active individuals. Look for plant-based or whey protein depending on your dietary preferences.
  • Vitamins: Multivitamins can provide a broad spectrum of essential nutrients to support overall health.
  • Omega-3 Fatty Acids: Great for heart health, brain function, and reducing inflammation. Fish oil or algae-based supplements are excellent choices.
  • Probiotics: Support gut health and improve digestion, which is foundational for overall wellness.
  • Collagen: Promotes skin, hair, and joint health, especially beneficial as you age.

How to Choose Quality Products

  • Check Certifications: Look for certifications like USDA Organic, Non-GMO Project Verified, or third-party testing to ensure purity and quality.
  • Read Ingredients: Avoid products with artificial additives, fillers, or allergens that don’t align with your needs.
  • Research the Brand: Choose brands with a strong reputation for transparency and quality, like PinkWellnessROI.
  • Consult Professionals: Speak with a healthcare provider to ensure the supplements fit your health goals.
