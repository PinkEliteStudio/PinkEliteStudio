2. Discovery Process: Uncovering Challenges

2.1 Audit Current Performance

  1. Log into Google Analytics to export data on traffic, bounce rates, and conversions.
  2. Review ad platforms (Meta, Google Ads) for cost-per-click, ROAS, and conversion rates.
  3. Install Hotjar to analyze user behavior (e.g., clicks, scrolls) on your website.
  4. Document findings in the KPI spreadsheet (Section 12).

2.2 Identify Pain Points

  1. Conduct a SWOT analysis to identify weaknesses (e.g., high cart abandonment).
  2. Create a Typeform survey asking customers about purchase barriers (e.g., price, trust).
  3. Map your funnel in a tool like Lucidchart to spot drop-offs (e.g., low email opens).
  4. List top 3 pain points to address in your growth plan.

2.3 Set SMART Goals

  1. Define Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound goals (e.g., "Increase email open rates to 25% in 90 days").
  2. Calculate customer lifetime value (CLV) using Shopify reports to align goals.
  3. Log goals in the KPI spreadsheet and review weekly.

3. Understanding ROI

3.1 What is ROI?

Return on Investment (ROI) measures campaign profitability: (Revenue from Campaign - Campaign Cost) / Campaign Cost × 100. Example: $500 Meta Ads spend generating $2,500 sales = 400% ROI.

3.2 How to Calculate and Optimize ROI

  1. Track revenue in Google Analytics or ad platforms (e.g., Meta Ads Manager).
  2. Log costs: ad spend, software (e.g., MailerLite $9/month), creative expenses.
  3. Use KPI spreadsheet to calculate ROI weekly.
  4. Pause campaigns with ROI < 200%; scale those > 400%.
  5. A/B test ad copy, audiences, and landing pages to boost ROAS.

4. SEO for Wellness Brands

4.1 What is SEO?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) improves your website’s ranking on Google, driving organic traffic. For wellness brands, SEO targets high-intent keywords (e.g., "best organic supplements") to attract customers without ad spend.

4.2 Step-by-Step SEO Implementation

  1. Keyword Research:
    • Sign up for SEMrush (free trial) or Google Keyword Planner.
    • Search for keywords like "organic supplements" (1K–10K monthly searches, low difficulty).
    • Select 10 primary keywords (e.g., "vegan protein powder") and 20 long-tail (e.g., "best vegan protein powder for women").
    • Save keywords in a Google Sheet for tracking.
  2. On-Page SEO:
    • Meta Descriptions with Keywords:
      • Write 160-character descriptions including primary keyword (e.g., "Shop the best organic supplements for wellness. Free shipping on orders $50+!").
      • Edit in Shopify: Products > Edit > Search Engine Listing Preview.
      • Check existing descriptions in Google Search Console: Performance > Search Results > Pages.
    • Headers (H1, H2, H3):
      • Use one H1 per page with primary keyword (e.g., "Best Organic Supplements").
      • Add 2–4 H2s for subtopics (e.g., "Why Choose Our Supplements?").
      • Include keywords naturally in headers to signal relevance to Google.
      • Edit headers in Shopify’s page editor or theme code.
    • Optimize title tags (60 characters) with keywords (e.g., "Organic Supplements | [YourBrand]").
    • Add alt text to images (e.g., "vegan protein powder bottle").
  3. Technical SEO:
    • Submit sitemap.xml via Google Search Console (find in Shopify: Settings > Files).
    • Test site speed on PageSpeed Insights; aim for 90+/100 (use Shopify’s Optimize Images feature).
    • Use Screaming Frog (free version) to find broken links; fix in Shopify.
    • Ensure mobile-friendliness via Google’s Mobile-Friendly Test.
  4. Content Creation:
    • Write 2 blog posts/week (1,500–2,000 words) targeting primary keywords.
    • Example: "10 Benefits of Vegan Protein Powder" with H2s like "Supports Muscle Growth."
    • Share posts on Pinterest and Meta with keyword-rich captions.
  5. Link Building:
    • Guest Posts: Email 10 wellness blogs (e.g., MindBodyGreen) monthly, offering a 1,000-word article (e.g., "Top Wellness Trends for 2025") with a link to your site.
    • Influencer Partnerships: Collaborate with 2–3 micro-influencers (10K–50K followers) to create co-branded content linking to your product pages.
    • Directory Listings: Submit your site to niche directories (e.g., WellnessMama directory).
    • Forum Participation: Answer questions on Reddit (r/Supplements) or Quora, linking to your blog posts where relevant.
    • Track backlinks monthly using Ahrefs’ free Backlink Checker.
  6. Track Performance:
    • Log into Google Search Console weekly to monitor:
    • Clicks: Number of clicks from search results (aim for 10% monthly growth).
    • Impressions: Times your site appeared in searches (track trends).
    • Average Position: Keyword rankings (aim for top 10 for 5 keywords in 6 months).
    • Use Google Analytics to track organic traffic and conversions (e.g., purchases, sign-ups).
    • Set up a Google Data Studio dashboard to visualize clicks, impressions, and revenue.
    • Review rankings monthly in SEMrush; adjust content for underperforming keywords.
    • Document metrics in the KPI spreadsheet (Section 12).

5. Building a Proven Marketing Funnel

5.1 Funnel Structure

  • TOFU: Awareness via blog posts, Meta Ads, Pinterest Pins.
  • MOFU: Consideration via email sequences, webinars, quizzes.
  • BOFU: Conversion via retargeting ads, limited-time discounts.

5.2 Step-by-Step Funnel Creation

  1. Create a lead magnet (see Section 6.2 for template).
  2. Build a Netlify landing page to capture emails (Section 10).
  3. Set up a 5-email nurture sequence in MailerLite (Section 9).
  4. Launch Meta retargeting ads for MOFU leads (budget: $10/day).
  5. Send a 15% discount email to BOFU leads.
  6. Track funnel conversions in Google Analytics (e.g., email sign-ups, purchases).

6. Google Ads for High-ROI

6.1 Campaign Setup

  1. Create a Google Ads account at ads.google.com and link to Google Analytics.
  2. Set up a Search campaign for high-intent keywords and a Remarketing campaign for site visitors.
  3. Create ad groups by product categories (e.g., supplements, yoga gear).
  4. Set a daily budget of $20–$50, adjusting based on ROI.

6.2 Ad Creation and Optimization

  1. Keyword Bidding:
    • Use Google Keyword Planner to find high-intent keywords (e.g., "buy organic supplements," "vegan yoga mats").
    • Set bids for exact match keywords (e.g., $1–$3 per click).
    • Add negative keywords (e.g., "free," "cheap") to avoid irrelevant clicks.
  2. Ad Copy Creation:
    • Write 3 ads per ad group using templates (Section 13).
    • Example: Headline: "Shop Premium Supplements" | Description: "Save 20% on organic supplements. Free shipping $50+!" | CTA: Shop Now.
    • Include ad extensions (e.g., site links: "Shop Supplements," "View Yoga Gear").
    • Use dynamic keyword insertion for relevance (e.g., {KeyWord:Supplements}).
  3. Lead Magnet Integration:
    • Create a lead magnet to capture emails (e.g., "Free Supplement Guide").
    • The lead magnet PDF is available at https://pinkwellnessroi.com/lead_magnet.pdf.
    • Promote via a Display ad linking to a Netlify landing page.
  4. Optimization:
    • Install conversion tracking for purchases and sign-ups (Google Ads > Tools > Conversions).
    • A/B test ad headlines and descriptions weekly.
    • Pause ads with click-through rates < 1% or cost-per-conversion > $20.
    • Scale ads with ROAS > 4:1 by increasing budget 20% weekly.
