Pink Wellness ROI

High-Ticket Client Acquisition Guide

Introduction

Transform Pink Wellness ROI into a premier digital marketing agency for wellness e-commerce brands. With Rhina Delgado’s 7+ years in real estate, networking, and ads, this guide, inspired by Alex Schlinsky’s Prospecting On Demand, provides a roadmap to land high-ticket clients ($3K+/month) who need your AI-driven solutions. No technical skills required—follow this plan to replace your income and scale.

1. Craft an Irresistible Agency Model

1.1 Niche & Positioning

  • Niche: Wellness e-commerce brands with $100K+ revenue (e.g., supplements, yoga gear).
  • USP: AI-driven marketing guaranteeing 2x ROI, no client tech expertise needed.
  • Model: Done-For-You services with AI arbitrage and DaaS.

1.2 Core Services

  • AI Arbitrage Marketing: Optimize Meta, Google, Pinterest ads.
  • DaaS Solutions: Real-time insights via Vendasta.
  • High-Ticket Client Acquisition: Land $3K+ clients.
  • SEO & Content: Boost visibility with blogs/videos.

1.3 Step-by-Step Setup

Step 1: Search LinkedIn for “wellness CEO” and Shopify for brands with $100K+ revenue.

Step 2: List pain points (e.g., high ad costs) in a Google Doc, note AI solutions.

Step 3: Set pricing at $3K-$5K/month, emphasizing 2x ROI.

Step 4: Create a Canva pitch deck with niche, USP, services.

Step 5: Get feedback on your pitch from a trusted contact.

4. Build an AI Arbitrage System

4.1 Tools

  • Smartly.io: Automates ad testing ($500-$1,000/month).
  • AdCreative.ai: Generates ad copy/visuals ($29-$149/month).
  • Google Ads AI: Automated bidding (free).

4.2 Delivery Process

  • Onboard with assets/goals.
  • Launch AI campaigns.
  • Monitor via Vendasta.
  • Deliver reports.

4.3 Step-by-Step Setup

Step 1: Sign up for Smartly.io, AdCreative.ai trials.

Step 2: Create Google Ads account, enable “Maximize Conversions.”

Step 3: Create Google Form for assets (logos, images).

Step 4: In AdCreative.ai, upload assets, select “Wellness,” generate 5 creatives.

Step 5: In Smartly.io, connect Meta/Google, launch campaigns.

Step 6: Check Vendasta analytics weekly.

Step 7: Email Vendasta reports monthly.

5. Land High-Ticket Clients (Schlinsky’s Method)

5.1 Acquisition Strategies

  • Lead Magnet: Offer this guide to capture leads.
  • High-Volume Outreach: Send 50 LinkedIn messages/day.
  • Refined Offer: Guarantee 2x ROI in 90 days.
  • Case Studies: Showcase wins (e.g., “Doubled [Client]’s Sales”).
  • One-Call Close: Use Schlinsky’s 4 W’s: Where now? Where to? Obstacle? Urgency?

5.2 Client Qualification

  • Target $100K+ revenue, $10K+/month ad budgets.
  • Use website form to filter leads.

5.3 Step-by-Step Roadmap

Step 1: Sign up for LinkedIn Sales Navigator ($79/month).

Step 2: Search “wellness CEO,” filter for $100K+ revenue.

Step 3: Send 50 messages/day: “Hi [Name], I doubled [Client]’s ROI with AI ads. Free audit?”

Step 4: Track responses in Google Sheet (Name, Brand, Date, Status).

Step 5: Set up Calendly (calendly.com/pinkelitestudio).

Step 6: Present Vendasta audits, use 4 W’s, offer 2x ROI.

Step 7: Create Canva case study for first win.

Step 8: Follow up with non-responders via MailerLite after 3 days.
